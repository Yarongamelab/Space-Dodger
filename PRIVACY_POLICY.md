# Privacy Policy for Space Dodger

**Last updated: October 2023**

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you install and use the **Space Dodger** mobile application ("the App").

## 1. Information We Collect
The App is built using the Flutter framework and includes third-party services.

### a. Automatically Collected Information
When you use the App, certain information is collected automatically by third-party services:
*   **Google AdMob:** We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. Google may collect and use your advertising ID (e.g., AAID on Android) and other device identifiers to provide personalized ads and for analytics purposes. 
*   **Analytics:** We may use basic analytics to understand how many users are playing the game.

### b. Local Data Storage
The App saves your game progress and high scores locally on your device using `shared_preferences`. This data stays on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

## 2. How We Use Your Information
We use the information collected to:
*   Provide and maintain the game.
*   Display advertisements to support the free version of the App.
*   Improve the game experience based on usage patterns.

## 3. Third-Party Services
The App uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you.
*   [Google Play Services](https://www.google.com/policies/privacy/)
*   [AdMob](https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543?hl=en)

## 4. Children’s Privacy
Our game is intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

## 5. Your Rights
Depending on your location (e.g., GDPR in the EU or CCPA in California), you may have rights regarding your data. Since we do not store personal data on our own servers, you can "reset" your data by clearing the App's cache/data or uninstalling the App.
